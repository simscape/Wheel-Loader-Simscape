Model

This example models a driveline with an articulation joint. The articulation joint can be configured to be a universal joint or a constant velocity joint

Open Model

Simulation Results

The input shaft is rotated at at constant speed. The bend angle of the shaft is increased. The test with the universal joint shows that the speed of the output shaft varies. The test with the constant velocity joint shows that the output speed does not vary.
